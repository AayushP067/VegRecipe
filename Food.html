Two person 3 meal Cooking recipes

Kichidi

  1. 2 Tbsp Oil
  2. 1 Cinnamon bark
  3. 1 Bayleaf
  4. 2 Tbsp Cumin seeds (Jeera)
  5. 1/3 Tbsp asafetida powder
  6. Vegetables
    • 3 Tomatoes or 2 canned Tomatoes
    • 3 large potatoes or 2 canned potatoes
    • 2 carrots(optional)
  7. Spices
    • 1/3 Tbsp chilli powder
    • 1 Tbsp salt
    • 1/2 Tbsp turmeric
  8. 3 cups rice (soaked 15 mins)
  9. 2 cups dry split pigeon peas(toor dal) (soaked for 15 mins)
  10. 9 cups water
Note: Stir occassionally, or after every 2 steps to prevent burning.
  1. Switch on the Instant pot and select 'Saute mode'
  2. Pour the oil. Once the oil is warm, add the Cinnamon bark and Bayleaf into the oil.
  3. After 10 seconds, add cumin seeds and asafetida powder.
  4. Wait until the cumin seeds have turned light brown, add the tomatoes, potatoes, and carrots.
  5. Once the oil has surfaced, add the spices and mix thoroughly.
  6. After 3 mins, add the rice, lentil and water.
  7. Close the instant pot.
  8. Select 'Cancel', and select 'Pressure cook' with Pressure level set 'High' and timer of 12 mins.
  9. After 12 mins, wait for 15 mins and then slowly let the pressure out.
